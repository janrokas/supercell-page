Create a team with your friends and start the brawl! Choose your favorite game mode and jump in this quick and fun game. You will be able to choose from a huge variety of brawlers that are created to match every kinf of gameplay. Destroy, shoot, explode and make your way to that epic VICTORY!



Pick your best brawlers and jump into your favorite maps!

Made by the creators of Clash Of Clans and Clash Royle, we present you - Brawl stars! A new free multiplayer Action game on iOS and Android. The game itself has an option to play in team or alone. You can also choose from a couple of game modes. All of the game modes and maps are changed very often so you might need to try out differnet brawlers on differnet maps. You can also get new brawlers by collecting trophies or unlocking them in the boxes. There are different rarities of the brawlers which makes it more difficult to get all of the fighters in a few boxes. After some time you will be able to upgrade your favorite brawlers with the tokens you collect. We wish you good luck with those boxes!
