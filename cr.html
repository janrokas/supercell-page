Find friends and team up in the CLAN WARS!

One of the best features in Clash Royale is clans. Make your own or join one and start the WAR! Clan War is a huge battle between 5 clans. It can be started by co-leader or a leader of the clan. It starts of with collection day where all of the clan members have to collect cards for their clan by playing different game modes. The upcoming day is called WAR DAY! Every single person in all of the 5 clans must make a war deck out of the cards they have collected before. Everyone has one chance, and they have to enter the final battle in which one of the clashers will get a medal for their clan. At the end of a war, the clans will be ranked from first to last depending on their medal count. The clans either get trophies and enter higher clan war arenas or lose some of their trophies.
